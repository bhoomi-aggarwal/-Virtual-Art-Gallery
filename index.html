Artwork 2: Vibrant Reggae Waves

Concentric circles and undulating waves pulse in vibrant reggae colors of red, gold, and green, creating a rhythmic tapestry that captures music's visual essence. Woven fiber textures reminiscent of traditional Jamaican textiles provide tactile depth, while varying densities mimic sound frequencies in motion. Circular motifs inspired by speaker systems radiate outward in harmonious patterns, their curvilinear forms flowing like melody lines across the composition. Natural shadows accentuate the dimensional quality, highlighting the contrast between smooth wave patterns and richly textured surfaces in this dynamic, energy-filled design.
